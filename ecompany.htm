LiLEDs (Shanghai) Company has the world's state-of-art optical design capabilities, combined with 20 years of automotive lighting and LED lighting experience, research, develop and product high efficient near-field optical lens, free form lens, Cosine lens, non-symmetric lens, total internal reflection Fresnel lens and prism, widely applied for LED lighting products. Meanwhile, LiLEDs (Shanghai) Company has a strong R&D team of electronic engineers, structural engineers, thermal analysis engineers, process engineers, quality engineers, tooling and injection molding engineers.

LiLEDs (Shanghai) Company has a Photometric Lab, Electronics Lab, Environmental Lab, Metrology Lab. The Company has Goniometor, Spectrum Meter, LED Intensity distribution tester, Integrating Spheres, High-pot tester, Power Factor, Harmonic Distortion testers, and environmental laboratory equipments for R&D , Product Development and Quality Assurance.

LiLEDs (Shanghai) Company has developed, manufactured Patent Pending LED Street lamps, LED Tunnel lamps, LED Industrial High-bay & Low-Bay lamps, LED Gas Stations Canopy Lamps, LED Wallwasher lamps, LED Garden lamps and as well as LED Interior lamps. All the products had been exported to Europe and U.S. markets.
